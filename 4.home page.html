Welcome to PennyThrift

A financial budget planner app helps users manage their finances by tracking both income and expenses. It allows users to set up a personalized budget, allocating specific amounts for various spending categories. The website provides insights into spending habits, helping users identify areas where they can save. It also allows users to set and track savings goals, ensuring they stay on target.

Purpose:

To help students and working professionals manage their finances efficiently.

The Problem:

Many students and professionals struggle with budgeting.

Proposed Solution:

A user-friendly platform to track and plan expenses, savings, and more.
