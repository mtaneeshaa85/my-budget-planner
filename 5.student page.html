Student Page
Purpose
This budget planning project is particularly useful for students as it helps them track their spending patterns and make more informed financial decisions. By analyzing their previous week's expenditures, the tool provides a personalized budget that aligns with their needs, helping them save money and avoid overspending. Additionally, it promotes financial responsibility, an essential skill for students managing limited resources while balancing academic and personal expenses.
Weekly Plan Approach
The given plan will be on a weekly basis so that students can easily keep a progress of their financial habits.
